:--_____________:--

Our school buses operate on specific routes, depending on the number of students on the routes. While we try our utmost,
it may not always be possible, to drop students at the door-step of their homes.


On finalization of the route, no changes or diversions will be entertained,
and parents are requested to cooperate. The Transport Manager may be contacted regarding any transport matter.

their wards by private auto or any other means should come intime, If any thing happens before school or after school hour school is not responsible for that.

Library Rules

:--_____________:--

Students are encouraged to utilize the library extensively for their reference and to enjoy reading.
One book at a time may be loaned to the student which could be retained for a week and returned promptly on the due date.
The students are expected to make a record of the books borrowed and read from the school library, in their diaries.
Careful handling of the book with no tearing off of pages or underlining is essential.
If any damage is noticed, the book must be replaced by the concerned student.
